About Us

DTU Consulting group was established in 2018 by likeminded enthusiasts. The society aims to foster a consulting culture among engineering undergraduates through multiple avenues helping students gain industry knowledge and build on their commercial acumen through case-solving practices, small-scale projects, alumni talks and networking.
